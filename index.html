Calendars for SIO Students

This is a temporary website for displaying and adding entries to calendars that list opportunities of relevance to SIO students. Hopefully this is a way to all give each other a boost in our graduate studies and beyond.

The Funding Opportunities calendar lists application deadlines for fellowships (graduate and postdoctoral), one-time scholarship awards, and grants for research, fieldwork, conference travel, and dissertation writing.

The Professional Development calendar lists events that students can attend, such as conferences, workshops, short courses and summer schools, local seminars, and webinars.

If you are interested in managing the events for these calendars, let Soli know at esmgarcia@gmail.com. If the embedded forms and calendars below are not displaying on your device, you may follow these direct links instead.
