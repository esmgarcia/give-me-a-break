GMT 5 Tutorial July 9-10, 2013

Visiting Prof. Paul Wessel will lead an informal workshop on the latest verion of Generic Mapping Tools (GMT 5), open to all interested at IGPP and SIO. It will be held in the Munk conference room (IGPP 303) on Tuesday, July 9 and Wednesday, July 10. Please find the proposed schedule for the workshop below. Read more about GMT 5 on the official website.

Participants are expected to bring their own computers with a working installation of GMT 5. Detailed instructions for installing GMT 5 are listed here.

There are a few optional sessions at the start and end of the workshop, and you may attend only selected sessions in between as you wish. If you are interested in attending the workshop, please sign up through this form. (alternate link)
